# Coming Soon

Features on the roadmap but not yet in production.

## AI Phone Receptionist

24/7 call answering powered by AI. Customers call your shop, the AI receptionist answers — schedules appointments, answers questions about their vehicle, and creates estimates from phone conversations. Never miss a call again.

**Status:** Planned. Waiting on Twilio integration + EIN.

## Voice-to-Estimate

Technicians narrate their findings and the AI builds the estimate in real time. No typing, no clicking — just talk through what the vehicle needs and automotAI builds the quote. Works hand-in-hand with the AI Phone Receptionist for customer-facing calls.

**Status:** Planned. Dependent on AI Phone Receptionist.

## Automated Customer Communication

SMS and email follow-ups for repair updates, estimate reminders, appointment confirmations, and post-service check-ins. Customers stay informed without you lifting a finger.

**Status:** Planned. Email reminders are partially live (appointment confirmations). SMS waiting on Twilio integration + EIN.

## Custom Shop Subdomains

Each shop gets its own branded subdomain — `shopname.automotai.com`. Customers visit the shop's custom URL to access the portal, book appointments, and pay invoices. White-label experience without the white-label complexity.

**Status:** Planned. Requires wildcard DNS + SSL cert setup on Cloudflare.
